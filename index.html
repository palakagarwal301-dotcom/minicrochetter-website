Mini Crochetter is a one-person handmade studio. Every coaster, bootie, plushie, and flower is crocheted by hand — no machines, no shortcuts.

I believe handmade gifts carry something that store-bought things simply can't — time, intention, and a little piece of the maker's heart.

Whether you're ordering a gift for someone special, or learning to crochet yourself, I want every experience with Mini Crochetter to feel warm, personal, and memorable.
